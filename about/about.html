~ About ~

Seth M.R. Jaipuria Schools is a group of schools run by the Seth M.R. Jaipuria Group. The schools draw their name from 1971 Padma Bhushan awardee Mungtu Ram Jaipuria, a philanthropist and industrialist who played a dominant role in the industrialization of the nation after independence.Seth M.R. Jaipuria School, New Delhi is affiliated to the Indian Council for Secondary Examinations, New Delhi.The school imparts education from the Pre-Primary level to class XII. In classes XI the school offers three potential streams i.e. Science, Commerce and Humanities.It aims at the all round development of the child and strives to mould them into sincere, dedicated, distinguished and morally sound citizens of the country.

~ Infrastructure ~

~ History ~

The Jaipuria Group's history in education began in 1945 with the establishment of Seth Anandram Jaipuria Commerce College in Kolkata. The college is still operational today and has around 7,000 students.The group's history in K-12 education began in 1974 with the establishment of Seth Anandram Jaipuria School in Kanpur.
